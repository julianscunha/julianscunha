## 🧠 About Me

Technology Consultant operating within the **Veeam VCSP partner ecosystem**, focused on data protection sizing, cost modeling, and internal tooling for enterprise customers. Builds production-grade Python/FastAPI applications that translate complex licensing and pricing models (PPU-based) into clear, sales-ready decisions. Strong focus on **governance-driven engineering** — architecture decisions, naming standards, and documentation treated as first-class deliverables, not afterthoughts.

- 🔭 Currently building **Veeam Sizer Pro**, a FastAPI-based sizing & cost estimation platform for VBR, VBfM365, VBfAWS, VBfAzure and VBfSalesforce
- 🌱 Currently exploring AI-assisted card/data generation for a soccer match-management dashboard
- 👯 Open to collaborating on internal tooling, cloud cost modeling, and governance frameworks
- ⚡ Fun fact: São Paulo FC supporter ⚽
